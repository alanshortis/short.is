In New York:

- **Soho:** South of Houston Street
- **Noho:** North of Houston Street
- **Tribeca:** Triangle Below Canal Street

In London:

- **Soho:** Possibly a hunting cry used by James Scott, the 1st Duke of Monmouth, as Soho was once an area where people would hunt
