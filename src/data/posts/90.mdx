title: 'On breakage and beauty'
---

In Japan the art of Kintsugi (金継ぎ), golden joinery, is where broken ceramics are repaired with lacquer mixed with gold, silver or platinum. The fixed item is considered more precious and beautiful than before, its breakage being part of the history of the item and not a flaw to disguise.
